Meet Anaiah AKA Papaya
As a girl who grew up dancing, singing, acting, and modeling I guess I might as well have added photography to the list! I was the kid who had a camera with her at all times and took way too many selfies with her friends. Now its something that has become one of my favorite creative outlets. I do it for the art and the collaboration. I am constantly inspired by the beautiful artists that I have met since living in LA. This leads me to creating beautiful conceptual work based off of the essence each artist holds. All of my projects are an extension of both me and the model combined. I am a self taught and hand-me-down equipment sort of girl. However, here I am making things that I never would've dreamed I would be making. And that's all thanks to art!
